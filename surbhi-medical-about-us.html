About Us

We are located at Jankalyan hall, Astron chowk. We provide almost all type of medicine and mostly all the cancer caring drugs. Effectiveness is our goal. Efficiency is our strength. We do believe that helping our customers is as good as helping you as it gives us satisfaction. Our main motto is to serve and satisfy its customers to the utmost providing our customers the right medical aid when they need it most. We desire to achieve customer fulfillment and satisfaction through their pleasant experience. This helps us ensure that the drugs we sell are not just inexpensive, but also conform to world-class standards of manufacturing and quality control.

The bond of mutual trust and belief, our customers and we have in one another has been smooth and just got better with each passing day. It is almost something of a rapid-fire trust, an implicit one and one, which gets wider with each of this relationship. We hope the same effect plays up when our esteemed readers cull through the information trivia that follows ahead.We aim to improve upon our work approach, as such we invite our patrons to put forward their viewpoints to us vide our eMail ID. We hope that we will continue to sail with one another ahead in the same manner as we did all these years.

The year 1982 is termed as the dawn of the information age; it heralded the leap of the world into a whole New World of technology and its vast potential. It was precisely in this year that we entered the field of retail medical business. We did so in the area of Musheerabad, Hyderabad the city of historic Charminar and head quarters of the pharma legends like Dr Reddy's Laboratories, Natco Pharma, Matrix Labs, etc. which is a buzz with medical shops dime a dozen and the competition is intense. The survival instinct has not come about easily, but through a series of factors as commitment, dedication, sincerity, hard work, single-minded focus towards work and a deep sense of respect for customer's welfare. We also keep ourselves abreast of the latest of developments in the field of medicine; utilise its finer points and benefits to our customer's advantage. The switch over of information to online format is one such step.
